Sub-micron Particle Characterization via Digital Holography

Technique for characterizing sub-micron weak phase particles using reconstructed volume intensities from in-line digital holography microscopy, with non-linear fitting and machine learning for precise particle size and refractive index estimation.

Sep. Purif. Technol. · 2024

Acoustically Enhanced Porous Media Filtration

Multiphysics model showing how standing acoustic waves improve fiber filter capture efficiency by up to 123× through acoustic radiation forces, with staggered fiber arrangements providing optimal enhancement for micro/nanoparticle air purification.

Int. J. Pharm. · 2021

Schlieren Monitoring of Pharmaceutical Heterogeneity

Portable all-lens Schlieren setup for real-time visualization and quantification of concentration heterogeneities in therapeutic protein and antibody solutions (BSA, IgG) during freeze–thaw cycling, extracting concentration gradients from light intensity variations.

Numerical demonstration of ring, protuberant, and crescent-shaped acoustic pressure nodes generated by circular scatterers in BAW devices, enabling programmable multi-node particle manipulation for biomedical acoustofluidics applications.
